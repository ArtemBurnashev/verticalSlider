Transitional

What makes transitional interior design ideas stand on their own are the combinations of tried & true décor styling utilizing traditional furnishings mixed with modern elements that keep a room from looking like too much of one particular style.

Scandinavian

This style has a deep appreciation for light woods, decluttered spaces, simple accessories and the use of natural and accent lighting and let’s not forget the little hints greenery.

MID-CENTURY MODERN

Defined by clean lines, organic shapes, minimal ornamentation and high functionality, this style undoubtedly has a timeless appeal.

Loft

A loft is a residential building characterized by a unique open space with no partition walls and very high ceiling that is generally built from former disused industrial buildings or commercial spaces.
